Society of Vibration & Acoustics Malaysia (SVAM)
Registration Number: 2121-11-SE
Officially registered on the 28th April 2011 and it was approved by the Malaysian Establishment Act 1966 under establishment regulations 1984. Based in UKM, SVAM also collaborated with various local universities as well as relevant industries partners related to sound and vibration applications.

Welcome to the Official SVAM page - Bridging Industries & Specialist for various Vibration & Acoustics Applications

Main Objectives
To promote local and international development and collaboration in all fields of vibration & acoustics including research, development, education, and standardisation.

To increase information exchange by sponsoring, cosponsoring or supporting seminars, workshops, congresses and publishing journals, newsletters and other publications
